hi! welcome to the battle game wiki!




stats and basic moves: (please note that there are no limits to how far these stats can go. this is a choice not something i'm working on.)

  • health: pretty self explanatory. if it drops below 0 you die.
  • energy: almost everything you do is multiplied by energy.
  • defense: all damage is subtracted from damage(e.g. damage(10) - defense(5) = total(5)).
  • attack: does 20 - 40 damage.
  • heal: heals you 25 - 40 health.
  • defend: gain 1 - 10 defence.
  • rest: heals you 10 health and gain .05 energy
  • skip: skips your turn.

    • characters:
  • spiderman: one of the original characters(marked original. i don't change these much).
    1. web shooter: does 40 - 50 damage but can only be used 4 times.
    2. face punch: does 30 - 45 damage.
    3. flying kick: 75% chance to do 35 - 60 and 25% chance to do 20 - 25.
    4. think: increases energy by .2 but if energy is greater than 2 you can't use it.
  • shobe: requested (i try not to change these much).
    1. cheese toss - does 30 damage and heals 15 health cheese wall - gains 7 defense and 10 health
    2. cheesy onslaught - does 69 damage (why 69? idk)
  • wyvern: requested. starts with 110 health and 10 defense.
    1. fire breath: does 20 - 30 damage and removes .05 energy from your opponent.
    2. claw swipe: does 40 - 70 damage.
  • thanos: original.
    1. smash: does 25 - 45 damage.
    2. thanos snap: has a 50% chance of doing 50 - 70 damage or does 50 damage to you.
    3. rewind: need to have above .5 energy, resets all stats that are under what you started with, and resets enemy stats if they have above the original stats but if they have 1.5 times your defense they have a 50% chance to resist and if they have 2.5 times your defense they have a 100% chance to resist.
    4. true heal: heals you 50 health.
    5. power stone shield: gives you 50 defense but can only be used once.
  • pikachu: original.
    1. thunder shock: does 40 - 50 damage but has a 25% chance to do 10 damage to you.
    2. tail slap: does 35 damage.
    3. pika block: does 5-10 damage and makes you immortal for one round.
    4. evolve: turns you into raichu and resets your health.
  • raichu: original. can only be accessed by using pikachu.
    1. thunder punch: does 45 - 55 damage.
    2. wild charge: does 50 - 60 but has a 50% chance of doing 10 - 25 damage to you.
    3. raichu block: same as pika block but does 10 - 15 damage.
    4. unevolve: resets your health to 100 and turns you into pikachu.
  • Hercules: original.
    1. arrow shots: has a 75% chance of doing 50 - 65 damage and a 25% chance of missing.
    2. club strike: does 35 damage.
    3. equalize heaths: if your health is lower than the enemy, your health becomes equal to your enemy.
  • jedi: original.
    1. lightsaber slash: renamed attack.
    2. force whirlwind: does 60 damage but can only be used twice.
    3. battlemind: increases your energy by .4 but if your energy is over 2, you can't use it.
