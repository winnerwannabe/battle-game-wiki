stats and basic moves: (please note that there are no limits to how far these stats can go. this is a choice not something i'm working on.)

  • health: pretty self explanatory. if it drops below 0 you die.
  • energy: almost everything you do is multiplied by energy.
  • defense: all damage is subtracted from damage(e.g. damage(10) - defense(5) = total(5)).
  • attack: does 20 - 40 damage.
  • heal: heals you 25 - 40 health.
  • defend: gain 1 - 10 defence.
  • rest: heals you 10 health and gain .05 energy
  • skip: skips your turn.
